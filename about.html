About KomTek

We Are Increasing Business Success With Technology

KomTek is a UK-based global provider of VoIP telephony, digital marketing, and web development — built on 15+ years of delivering operational excellence for businesses worldwide.

Our Story

A Trusted Technology Partner Since 2007

KomTek was founded with a single mission: to give businesses of every size access to enterprise-grade technology without the enterprise price tag. What started as a UK-focused VoIP provider has grown into a full-service technology company serving clients across the UK and globally.

Today, our 250+ team of telecoms engineers, digital marketers, web developers, and IT specialists work together to help businesses communicate better, market smarter, and operate more efficiently.

We're proud to be a performance leader in the telecommunications and IT industry — achieving operational excellence while keeping costs transparent and customer support genuinely responsive.
