Common Questions

How does the subscription work?

Once you complete the onboarding form, you'll be directed to set up a monthly Direct Debit via GoCardless. Your subscription begins once payment is confirmed and we'll be in touch within one business day to kick things off.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. All plans are on a rolling monthly contract. Simply give us 30 days' written notice by emailing info@komtek.co.uk and your subscription will end at the close of that notice period.

Are there any setup fees?

No. There are no hidden setup fees or onboarding charges. The monthly price is all-inclusive of strategy, management, and reporting.

Can I sign up for both services?

Absolutely. Many of our clients run SEO and PPC together for maximum visibility. Simply complete both onboarding forms and set up a separate Direct Debit for each service.
