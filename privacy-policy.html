Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 May 2026

1. Who we are

KomTek Ltd ("KomTek", "we", "us", "our") is a company registered in England and Wales. Our registered address is:

KomTek Ltd
8B Accommodation Road
Golders Green
London NW11 8ED

We are the data controller for personal data collected through this website. You can contact us about data protection matters at info@komtek.co.uk or by calling 033 33 05 6676.

2. What data we collect and why

a) Contact form enquiries

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Company name (optional)
  • Service of interest
  • Your message

Purpose: To respond to your enquiry and provide the information or service you requested.
Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) — it is in our and your mutual interest to respond to business enquiries.

b) Website analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. This service collects anonymised data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device type and browser
  • How you arrived at our site (search engine, direct, referral)

Purpose: To improve our website content and user experience.
Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR). Google Analytics data is anonymised and we do not use it to identify individual users.
Data processor: Google LLC. See Google's Privacy Policy.

c) Contact form processing (Web3Forms)

Our contact form is processed by Web3Forms, a third-party service. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to Web3Forms servers for delivery to us. Web3Forms does not store your data beyond what is needed for delivery. See Web3Forms Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies

Our website uses the following cookies:

Cookie Provider Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics Distinguishes unique users 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics Session state persistence 2 years

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use the website. For more information visit aboutcookies.org.

4. How long we keep your data

  • Contact form enquiries: We retain your enquiry details for up to 3 years for business and legal purposes, after which they are securely deleted.
  • Analytics data: Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default, after which it is automatically deleted.

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Web3Forms — for processing contact form submissions
  • Google LLC — for website analytics (anonymised)
  • IONOS SE — our web hosting provider, who stores the website files

All third-party processors are required to handle your data in accordance with UK GDPR.

6. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data where there is no lawful reason to retain it
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@komtek.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Our website is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Contact form data is transmitted securely to our email.

8. International transfers

Google Analytics data may be processed on servers outside the UK and EEA. Google LLC participates in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework and provides appropriate safeguards under UK GDPR Article 46. Web3Forms servers are located in the EU.

9. Links to other websites

Our website contains links to third-party websites (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and recommend you review their privacy policies separately.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. How to complain

If you are unhappy with how we handle your personal data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection regulator:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire SK9 5AF
Tel: 0303 123 1113

We would, however, appreciate the opportunity to address your concerns before you contact the ICO — please reach out to us first at info@komtek.co.uk.
